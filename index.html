02 / LOAD CASES

Central standards vs. local context

Create leverage without flattening domain semantics or forcing every use case through one brittle template.

Provider leverage vs. portability

Exploit cloud-native strengths while preserving architectural control over identity, data and evidence.

Speed vs. proof

No release is fast when evaluation and observability are deferred until failure.

Architecture authority vs. team ownership

The architect sets the building code; engineering teams still own what runs.

Human authority vs. agent autonomy

Actions need explicit boundaries, approval thresholds, rollback and traceability.

Stable platform vs. changing models

Standards must remain durable while providers, methods and expectations evolve.
